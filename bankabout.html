FEDERAL BANK

Federal Bank Limited is an Indian private sector bank headquartered in Aluva, Kochi.
The bank has 1,272 branches spread across different states in India.
It also has representative offices abroad at Abu Dhabi, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and Dubai.
